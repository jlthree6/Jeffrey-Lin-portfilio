Kodak Rivival


For the 2nd project in Communication Design I, we had to revive dead brands. My group chose to revive Kodak by turning it into an Instagram Competitor. I came with the idea of capturing moments using frames like poloroid frames, picture frames, hand frames, and a mobile phone. What I would want to fix is the lighting on the hands needs to match the settings.
