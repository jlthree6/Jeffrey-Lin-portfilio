Alternative Happy Vinyl Art


In Communication Design I, our class was tasked to make an alternative Vinyl art for the song that we chose. I chose the song "Happy" by Pharrell Williams because it was a song that I remembered listening to when i was young. The idea of the Vinyl art was to have the shape people do activities that made them happy. The shape people represent different races and kinds of people also seen in the Happy video.
